Easy Egg Recipe



Spicy Egg-Avocado Toast
Prep time
20mins
cook time
20 mins
Total time
40 mins

         SERVES:1

Ingredients

1 lg egg
1 slice whole-wheat bread
1/2 avocado, sliced
1 watermelon radish, thinly sliced
Hot sauce

Directions:

BRING a small saucepan with 2 1/2 inches of water to a boil. Add egg and reduce heat to a bare simmer. Cook 11 minutes, then run under cool water. Crack and peel, then thinly slice. TOAST 1 slice whole-wheat bread; top with avocado, egg slices, and radish, seasoning each layer. ADD a few dashes of hot sauce; serve
Serve Hot. Enjoy!

NUTRITION

(per serving) 256 cal, 11 g pro, 18 g carb, 7 g fiber, 2 g sugars, 16 g fat, 3 g sat fat, 196 mg sodium
